KaohsiungTaiwan MICE City
Taipei Taoyuan Taichung Tainan
INTRODUCTION
Long renowned as a hub of logistics and manufacturing industries, the Port of Kaohsiung has evolved and is now characterized by streams of visitors and intelligent services. The “Asia’s New Bay Area” project marks Kaohsiung’s initiative in developing towards industrial transformation, and the MICE industry, a newly-emerging industry in recent years, is one of the focal points. The well-established hardware and software related to MICE, along with Kaohsiung’s iconic image as a maritime city, have come to serve as a phenomenal force to attract more business opportunities for the City’s tourism and MICE industries. It is anticipated that the joint efforts by the government and all sectors of society will put Kaohsiung’s industrial, harbor and cost advantages into full play, thereby enabling it to join the lineup of the world’s major host cities for MICE events.

Kaohsiung Exhibition Center
The Kaohsiung Exhibition Center, situated at the heart of the Asia New Bay Area, is supported by a professional MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) team to facilitate various exhibitions, conferences, and events. In 2021, it elevated its status to become the nation's sole 5G-dedicated smart venue, incorporating the latest MICE technology.

Arts & Humanities
The Pier-2 Art Zone
The Pier-2 Art Zone, composed of retired port-side warehouses, is a popular attraction for both locals and tourists in Kaohsiung. Weiwuying National Art and Culture Center, Dadong Culture and Art Center, Kaohsiung City Cultural Center, Kaohsiung City Museum of Art, Neiwei Art Center, Kaohsiung General Library, and Kaohsiung Film Museum offer art, music, drama, performing arts and many library collections.

Seaside Hotspots
Rainbow Arch
Kaohsiung is surrounded by mountains and sea, formerly relying on the port for its livelihood. Nowadays, more seaside space has been opened up, with the Gushan-Cijin Ferry Station, Xizi Bay, and Qijin Beach providing access to Kaohsiung's waters, plus yacht rides that offer cultural history tours and solar-powered boat rides on the Love River that demonstrate smart driving technology. Hongmaogang Cultural Park has depictions of early fishermen lifestyles, and there one can also watch the magnificent scenery of big ships leaving the port.

Kaohsiung Music Center
The Kaohsiung Exhibition Center, Kaohsiung Port Tourism Center, Kaohsiung Pop Music Center, Kaohsiung E-sports Arena, and Kaohsiung Software Park jointly formed the Asia New Bay Area 5G AIoT Innovation Park to demonstrate the latest application technology. Dream Reality Moondream Reality, VR Somatosensory Theater VR Film Lab, and Holo Park Guangyu Floating Theater Life Square all incorporate AR/VR/MR technologies.
